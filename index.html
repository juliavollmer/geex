Gedankenexperiment

A *Gedankenexperiment* ( german for *thought experiment* ) describes a hypothetical setup or situation that is theoretically mapped out in order to think about consequences and outcomes. Although a traditional *Gedankenexperiment* is by its nature a fully virtual endeavor it can be a very powerful device to instigate thinking, provoke change, criticise assumptions, deliver a cautionary tale, optimistically propose better alternatives, illustrate implications of the relationship between technology, science, and humans, or simply entertain. But what if we were to combine these dissecting, analytical and narrative qualities of the *Gedankenexperiment* with the transformative and translational tools and methods of design?
